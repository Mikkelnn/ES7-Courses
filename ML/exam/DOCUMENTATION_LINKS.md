### What Covariance Values Mean

- Positive Covariance (Cov > 0): The variables tend to move in the same direction. When one variable's value increases, the other's also tends to increase, and vice versa.
- Negative Covariance (Cov < 0): The variables tend to move in opposite directions. When one variable's value increases, the other's tends to decrease.
- Zero Covariance (Cov â‰ˆ 0): There is no linear relationship between the variables; they don't consistently move together or apart.
